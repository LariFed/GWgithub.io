Please keep in mind the following:

It is helpful if the name of the submitted paper pdf is the names of the authors as a comma separated list, e.g. "Green, Tao.pdf".

It probably goes without saying that Inventiones has extremely strict acceptance criteria and the vast majority of submitted papers are rejected.



I aim to confirm receipt of your paper within 10 days of submission. If I don't, please don't hesitate to write again.
